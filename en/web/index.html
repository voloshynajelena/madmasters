Web development

Created by

The development of web-sites is a laborious process that requires competent planning, the result of which will be a resource that can bring profit to the company and / or adequately present its company and services.

+ Business card website

This is your virtual business card. It will allow potential customers to get acquainted with the company, its products and services, prices and contacts of the company. Contains up to 10 pages. Ideal for quickly starting a business.

A business card site is informative, concise, individual and attractive Design.

+ Corporate website

This is a kind of "face" of the company, its virtual representation on the Internet and an image tool. Such a site provides complete information about the company, its development history, goods and services, terms of cooperation, online services, open vacancies, news and everything else that reflects the company's activities. Special attention is paid to Design. It will correspond to the style of your company and inspire confidence among potential customers and partners.

A corporate website is a must have for medium and large businesses.

+ Website directory

Such a site focuses on the goods (services) of the company providing the most extensive information. It provides potential customers with round-the-clock access to price lists, a detailed description of products or services, a presentation of the assortment from the best sides. From the online store differs in the absence of a basket for a comprehensive order via the Internet.

+ E-shop

The online store site is perfect for companies that have decided to engage in Internet commerce. It will allow you to remotely advertise the goods, accept the order for purchase, delivery and payment.

With an online store, you get the opportunity to sell goods not only in the domestic market, but also to supply them abroad. The development of an online store will allow you to bring the company to the fastest growing market for selling via the Internet.

+ Landing page

This is a one-page target site that the user gets to when clicking on an advertisement. Landing page provides the visitor with only the information for which he came to this site (purchase of goods or services, receiving a contact from the customer, downloading a particular file and much more).

Other services

+ Create Distribution emails

Distribution emails are the foundation of marketing campaigns and the easiest way to keep in touch with customers. Using Distribution emails, you can greet new customers, announce future promotions, invite existing customers to join a loyalty program, or send special offers with a promo code.

+ Calculators and shapes

With the help of web-forms, you can create forms of any complexity, which will allow you to collect and process customer data. You can create an online survey, feedback forms, FAQ, pop-up forms (callback, size table, etc.), Calculators and others.

Features of Calculators and forms: calculation by formula, field dependency logic, step-by-step forms, images in Calculator, sending data to e-mail, SMS-alerts, etc.

+ Creating themes for CMS

In today's world of web technology, CMS is the basis for creating any website, from a simple personal blog to an online store, on most corporate websites. There are many templates for creating themes for CMS. But, since they are widely available, they are not unique. The Mad Masters team creates one-of-a-kind topics based on the wishes of the customer.

+ Multilingual sites

A multilingual site contains several language versions on one site. In the competitive environment of online business, a multilingual site is the most reasonable way to gain the trust of the client. Such sites allow potential customers to "read" your site in his native language, increases competitiveness and more opportunities for growth, as such a site becomes international.

+ Subdomain site

This is a branch from the main site. Most often used on large-scale portals and consist of large sections, which over time, for the convenience of access to them by visitors, can be separated into separate sites.

3 main purposes: for organizing a forum or a closed section of the site, for the mobile version, for training (for example, using a subdomain to test themes, checking the work of plugins, etc.).
