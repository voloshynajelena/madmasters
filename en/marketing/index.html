Online Marketing

Optimization, website promotion and advertising services

Internet marketing is SEO-promotion of the site, context, webinars, etc. The advantages of Internet marketing are considered to be interactivity, the possibility of mass appeal to a wide Auditory, the possibility of the most accurate targeting, the possibility of post-click analysis, the creation, updating and constant replenishment of the buyers' database. It is also important to understand the need for part of the target Auditory of goods (services).

+ Search Engine Website Optimization (SEO)

Search Engine Optimization is the most reliable and effective way to help potential customers quickly find your site in search engines. The higher the position of the site in search engines, the more often potential Clients come to it. Provides high performance in the long run.

+ Contextual advertising (PPC)

PPC advertising is a key element of Internet marketing. Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Distribution, Targeted advertising, etc. Also the most effective way to quickly attract a targeted auditory to a site that is suitable for any business.

The main task of contact advertising is to advertise goods, services or a website and quickly attract customers.

+ Targeted advertising (SMM)

Targeted advertising can also be called the management of social networks. It is promoted in those social networks where your target Auditory "lives". It matters: user preferences (they view videos or scroll through), user interest (watch the whole video or just the beginning), engagement (whether they like posts with video content, share them with friends, leave comments, etc.).

+ Audit and site analysis

In order for a web site to function without failures, it requires a certain "care" and attention. Before promoting the site, it is necessary to identify all its possible shortcomings.

Comprehensive complex analysis includes technical analysis of the site (loading speed, the presence of errors in the program code, etc.), seo Audit of the site (will help to understand what stops the resource in occupying the first lines in search engines), Audit Design of the web-resource (it must be flawless), Audit site usability (checks the comfort of using the site by potential customers), analysis of your competitors.

+ Analysis of competition, forecast and preparation of a promotion plan

A clear concept of site analysis and the subject of competitors' activities, designation of key competitors, analytics based on search results, site visibility analysis, creation of competent linking, Formation of a broad site structure, content optimization, improvement of site usability, external site optimization, increase in the conversion rate from visitors to customers, phased optimization of pages by sections, categories, implementation of improvements based on data analysis.
