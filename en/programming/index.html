Custom development

Creating Calculators, Forms, and Widgets

Custom development of sites may be needed as a separate service if the site already exists and some of its refinement, expansion of functionality is required.

+ Calculatorы

The main role of online Calculators is in obtaining by a potential client correct and reliable information on the calculation of the order of interest, without additional surveys and collection of customer information. Calculator on the functions of performing calculations should be understandable to the client and not be structurally difficult to handle, so as not to cause irritation of the visitor and leaving the page.

+ Shapes

The form allows you to establish feedback between the visitor of the web page and the web application.Depending on the wishes of the client, it can be made simple, complex, with and without animation, autofill form fields and so on.

+ Widgets

Widgets allow you to add and expand the functionality of the site. Non-interactive widgets will work autonomously, regardless of the consumer's desire, weather display, stock reports, clock). Widgets of social networks (buttons with logos of Facebook, Twitter, Instagram)Message widgets allow you to create the most convenient service on the company's website as simply and easily as possible. Any person registered in the social network through the widget can communicate with employees of your company, consult on important issues, clarify data, etc.

+ Animation

Animation is one of the basics of web design these days, it is a powerful tool that allows website visitors and customers to interact more easily with the product interface and achieve their goals faster.

Animation helps the user see the result of their actions, which can be a classic loading bar, a wireframe display of web pages (a space that gradually fills up with downloadable information), animation of controls, animated response to user actions, creative effects, and much more.

+ Layout of mailing lists

Layout of letters to the most popular email platforms: Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, Android, iOS, MozillaThunderbird, Microsoft Outlook, The Bat. Emails will look great both in the browser and in the mobile application.
